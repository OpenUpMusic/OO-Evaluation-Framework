Participant self-evaluation

Before you start...

Please ask each participant to answer the questions below. (You'll need to complete one questionnaire per participant.)

When you ask the questions, we suggest keeping it light and informal! Avoid asking questions at the end of a session - somewhere in the midst of music making often works well. If participants are not in a position to give a clear answer, please select 'not sure' and then add a comment to clarify.

Thank you!

Note: You need to complete this evaluation in one go as nothing gets submitted until you click Submit at the end.
