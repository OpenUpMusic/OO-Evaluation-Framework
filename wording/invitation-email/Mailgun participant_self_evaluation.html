It's now time for your Open Orchestras participants to complete their {{64156612__COL$E}} self-evaluations. The instructions below are for the music educator who will support them to do this.

Please click the links below (showing your participants’ initials) to reveal the questions and to record each participant’s answers. You may want to print out the questions to record their answers on paper first, before submitting them online.

We fully appreciate that Open Orchestras participants have a wide range of communication and/or learning difficulties. You will need to present these questions in an appropriate manner for each individual. If participants are not in a position to give a clear or definitive answer, please select "Don't know"; toward the end of the survey, there is space for you to add a comment to clarify.

When you ask participants these questions we suggest keeping it light, informal and avoid putting them on the spot! We have found that asking questions somewhere in the midst of music making works a lot better than at the end of a music session.

When you submit each survey you will be emailed a copy of the responses for your records.
